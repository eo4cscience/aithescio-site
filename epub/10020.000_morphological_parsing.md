# Morphological Parsing - Overview

TThis section of the web-site is meant to give you a solid introduction into the use of finite state transducers as the method for parsing the morphology of the Irish language. While we saw in the doumentation a small example of how FSTs transduce one string into another string, the reality of parsing the morphology of the Irish language is far more complex. 

To correctly parse the morphology of a language we need to sequence the translations through many layers of the language.This sequencing needs to be carefully designed and the integration of the transducer stages is a non-trivial matter.
