# Welcome to Aithesc.io Documentation

Aithesc.io bridges the gap between the formal morphological and syntactic
analysis of the Irish language and its underlying linguistic theory.

## Navigate the Linguistic Theory

Use the outline in the sidebar to explore topics:

- **Phonetics**: Understand the foundational sounds, including broad and slender
  distinctions, and initial mutations.
- **Morphology**: Delve into the inflectional systems of nouns and verbs.
- **Syntax**: (Coming Soon) Syntactic operations and parsing models.
