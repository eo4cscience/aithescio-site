# Morphology: <br><span style="font-size: 0.5em;">using the grammatical roles to form words</span>

The **Morphology** layer is the second stage in the morphotactics conversion. It takes the output of the morphophonology layer and assembles the next level of the words involved based on their grammatical role in the sentence. Like many langauges the role of a word (eg a genitive noun) changes the form of the word often by adding some extra morpheme.

In the case of the irish language these grammatical sroles are expressed by one of many suffixes.
